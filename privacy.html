Astro Burst – Privacy Policy

Overview

Astro Burst respects your privacy. The app is designed to function without collecting, storing, selling, or sharing personal data.

Camera Usage

Astro Burst uses your device’s camera for real-time hand tracking gameplay. Camera data is processed live on your device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted by Astro Burst.

Microphone Usage

Astro Burst may use your device’s microphone for real-time audio-reactive gameplay effects. Microphone input is processed live on your device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted by Astro Burst.

Data Collection

Astro Burst does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Third-Party Services

Astro Burst does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Children’s Privacy

Astro Burst does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Contact
